Once again Celestron surprises with a high quality product. Celestron belongs to the Xiaomi ecosystem, and is therefore a product of the usual high quality. Ideally for hobby star discoverers, or star photographers.

By means of a stainless steel tripod, it can be set up easily and everywhere without rusting. With the 6x24 Finder, star constellations and targets can be easily targeted before you can take a closer look at them with the different zoom levels.

The scope of delivery includes a 20 and 10 mm eyepiece, which offer 25 and 50x sizings. Furthermore, a connector is included, which allows a further 125x sizing. By means of a so-called teleconverter the zoom level can be doubled, so that the maximum sizing is 250x (125 x 2 = 250).
