How can I make a video call on my cell phone and work on my laptop at the same time?

The solution for students, employees and teachers to cope with the current situation. Do you often have to work on your cell phone and laptop at the same time and make video calls? Have you asked yourself how to hold your cell phone and type at the same time?

With this practical cell phone holder you can finally make phone calls and work on your laptop at the same time.

For example, the conference call on the cell phone can be made via zoom and typing on the laptop at the same time.

How does the whole thing hold?

You need a cover for your cell phone in which you attach a magnet. This magnet then adheres to the opposite magnets on the connector. Thus one can pull the cell phone also simply on and off without having to handle with “clamp mountings”.

In addition, you can leave the holder on the laptop, and can easily fold it in and out without having to mount it every time.
